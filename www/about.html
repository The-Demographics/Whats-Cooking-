About App

Summary

What's Cooking is an electronic cooking book. It is a great way to not only store your favourite recipes, but to also discover new ones too. Rather than having to search through a whole range of different cook books, you can simply search through our database of high quailty, user reviewed recipes at your leasure. You can even add your own!

While What's Cooking is aimed at students, anybody can use it. even your Gran!
